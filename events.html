Gayton Elementary School

January 30, 2024

Gayton Elementary School was kind enough to host the very first Code Critters workshop. This was a great experience with a fantastic group of fourth-grade and fifth-grade students.

Together, we delved into the inner workings of computers along with encryption and decryption. These young minds enthusiastically participated in a hands-on encryption-decryption activity, gaining a firsthand understanding of cybersecurity basics.

Expanding upon this experience, the students were also introduced to basic coding through Python, sparking curiosity and igniting their passion!
